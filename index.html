The Mission 开发初衷

Faced with stacks of complex lab reports, I often felt overwhelmed. Scattered data and fragile paper records were the starting point for Leukanote. I wanted a tool that, like a blank canvas ("Leukos"), could purely record health fluctuations.

I insist on keeping all data locally on your device and in your private iCloud. These records are more than data—they are a family's most private trust.

面对繁琐的化验单，我曾深感无助。零散的数据和易失的纸质记录，是「小白随记」诞生的起点。我希望打造一个像白纸（Leukos）一样纯净的工具，记录每一次健康波动。

我坚持将所有数据保留在您的本地设备和私有 iCloud 中。因为我知道，这些记录不仅是数据，更是每一位家庭成员最私密的托付。

Questions 常见问题

Where is my data? 数据存储在哪里？

Exclusively on your device and personal iCloud. We have no central servers and cannot access your health records. 数据仅存储在您的设备和私有 iCloud 中。我们没有中央服务器，无法访问您的任何健康记录。

How accurate is the OCR? 文字识别准确吗？

Recognition happens on-device using Apple Vision. While advanced, we recommend manual verification before saving. 识别功能基于 Apple Vision 技术在本地运行。虽然技术先进，但建议在保存前进行人工核对。
