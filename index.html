The Mission / 使命

Why Leukanote? 为什么开发「小白随记」？

Managing medical records shouldn't be a struggle. Faced with stacks of complex lab reports, I realized we needed a tool that acts like a blank canvas—simple, pure, and focused on what matters: your health trends.

Leukanote was built on two core principles: **Absolute Privacy** and **Visual Clarity**. By keeping all data on your device and iCloud, we ensure that your most sensitive information never leaves your hands.

管理医疗记录不该是一件痛苦的事。面对一叠叠复杂的化验单，我意识到我们需要一个像白纸一样纯粹的工具，去关注核心：您的健康趋势。

「小白随记」基于两个核心原则：**绝对隐私**与**视觉清晰**。通过将所有数据保留在您的设备和 iCloud 中，我们确保您最敏感的信息永远掌握在自己手中。

FAQ / 常见问题

General Questions 您可能想了解

🔒Is my data secure?我的数据安全吗？

Yes. All data is stored locally on your iPhone and synced via your private iCloud. We cannot access or see your records. 是的。所有数据都存储在您的 iPhone 本地，并通过您私有的 iCloud 同步。我们无法访问或查看您的任何记录。

📸How does OCR work?识别功能如何工作？

The app uses Apple's Vision framework to process images locally. No photos are ever uploaded to a server. 应用使用 Apple 的 Vision 框架在本地处理图片。没有任何照片会被上传到服务器。

☁️Can I sync across devices?可以多设备同步吗？

Absolutely. As long as you are signed into the same iCloud account, your data will sync automatically. 当然可以。只要您登录同一个 iCloud 账号，您的数据就会自动同步。

🗑️How do I delete my data?如何删除我的数据？

You can delete individual records anytime. To wipe everything, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app. 您可以随时删除单条记录。如需彻底清除，请在 App 内前往“设置 > 账号 > 注销账号”。

📶Does it work offline?离线可以使用吗？

Yes, the core recording and OCR features work perfectly without an internet connection. 可以，核心的记录和 OCR 识别功能在没有网络连接时也能完美运行。

📄Can I export my reports?可以导出报告吗？

We are working on PDF/Excel export features. Currently, you can view and screenshot your trend charts. 我们正在开发 PDF/Excel 导出功能。目前，您可以查看并截图您的趋势图表。
