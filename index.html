🌱 背后的故事 The Story

很多时候，面对一张张复杂的化验单，我们常常感到迷茫和无助。繁琐的数据、难以理解的指标、容易丢失的纸质单据……这正是我开发「小白随记」的初衷。

我希望有一个工具，能像一张白纸（Leukos）一样，清晰、纯净地记录下每一次健康的波动，用直观的趋势图表代替零散的数字。我坚持将所有数据保留在您的设备本地和 iCloud 中，因为我知道，这些数据不仅是记录，更是每一个家庭最私密的托付。

Faced with stacks of complex lab reports, I often felt overwhelmed and helpless. Scattered data and paper records that easily get lost—this was the starting point for Leukanote.

I wanted a tool that, like a blank canvas ("Leukos"), could clearly and purely record every health fluctuation, replacing fragmented numbers with intuitive trend charts. I insist on keeping all data locally on your device and in your private iCloud, because I know these records are more than just data—they are a family's most private trust.

❓ 常见问题 FAQ

我的数据安全吗？ Is my data secure?

您的所有健康数据均存储在设备本地或通过您私人的 Apple iCloud 进行加密同步。我们无法获取、查看或收集您的任何健康记录。

All your health data is stored locally on your device or encrypted via your private Apple iCloud. We cannot access, view, or collect any of your health records.

化验单识别准确吗？ How accurate is the lab report scanning?

文字识别在您的设备本地运行，图片不会上传到任何服务器。受限于图像质量，识别可能存在误差，请仔细核对后再保存。

Text recognition runs locally on your device; images are never uploaded to any server. Due to image quality limitations, recognition may have errors. Please verify carefully before saving.

如何删除我的账号？ How do I delete my account?

您可以在「我的」→「账号设置」→「注销账号」中申请删除账号。账号删除后，所有数据将被清除且无法恢复。

Go to "My" → "Account Settings" → "Delete Account". All data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
