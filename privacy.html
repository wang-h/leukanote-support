隐私政策 Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026年 Last updated: 2026

1. 核心隐私声明

【小白随记】是一款纯本地及 iCloud 同步的健康管理工具。我们极其重视您的隐私安全。

您的所有健康数据（化验单、指标、笔记等）均存储在您的设备本地，或通过您私人的 Apple iCloud 进行加密同步。

开发者（我们）无法获取、查看、收集或分析您的任何健康记录。

2. 信息收集与使用

身份验证：我们仅使用「通过 Apple 登录」(Sign in with Apple) 进行身份验证，获取您的匿名用户 ID 及可选的昵称，绝不会收集手机号等敏感信息。

本地 OCR 识别：当您扫描化验单时，文字识别完全在您的 iPhone 本地运行（基于 Apple Vision 框架），图片绝不会上传到任何第三方服务器。

系统权限：相机及相册权限仅用于本地文档扫描；通知权限仅用于在您的设备上发送本地用药提醒。

3. 信息的存储与分享

我们没有任何中央服务器来存储您的私人健康数据。

您的数据同步完全依赖于您自己的 Apple ID 及其关联的 iCloud 账户。

我们绝不会，也无法向任何第三方出售或共享您的健康信息。

4. 您的权利

您可以随时在 App 内或系统设置中修改、删除您的所有健康记录。

由于数据未存储在我们的服务器上，当您在设备上删除数据或卸载应用时，数据即被彻底清除。

1. Core Privacy Statement

Leukanote is a pure local and iCloud-synced health management tool. We take your privacy very seriously.

All your health data (lab reports, indicators, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device or encrypted via your private Apple iCloud.

The developer (we) cannot access, view, collect, or analyze any of your health records.

2. Information Collection & Use

Authentication: We only use "Sign in with Apple" for authentication, obtaining your anonymous user ID and optional nickname. We never collect sensitive information like phone numbers.

Local OCR: When you scan lab reports, text recognition runs entirely on your iPhone locally (based on Apple Vision framework). Images are never uploaded to any third-party server.

System Permissions: Camera and photo library permissions are only for local document scanning; notification permission is only for sending local medication reminders on your device.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

We do not have any central servers to store your private health data.

Your data sync depends entirely on your own Apple ID and associated iCloud account.

We will never, and cannot, sell or share your health information with any third party.

4. Your Rights

You can modify or delete all your health records anytime in the app or system settings.

Since data is not stored on our servers, when you delete data on your device or uninstall the app, the data is completely removed.
