Privacy Policy 隐私政策

Effective Date: April 12, 2026 生效日期：2026年4月12日

Your health records never leave your hands. We do not access, store, or sell your private data. 您的健康记录永远掌握在自己手中。我们不会获取、存储或出售您的私人数据。
01 / Data Storage

Data Storage & Encryption 数据存储与加密

All health data, lab reports, and indicators you enter are stored exclusively on your iPhone device and your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not use any centralized servers.

您输入的所有健康数据、化验单和指标仅存储在您的 iPhone 设备和您个人的 Apple iCloud 账户中。我们不使用任何中央服务器。

02 / Authentication

Sign in with Apple 账户与身份

We utilize "Sign in with Apple" for secure and anonymous authentication. We do not collect your real name, phone number, or residential address.

我们利用“通过 Apple 登录”进行安全且匿名的身份验证。我们不会收集您的真实姓名、手机号或家庭住址。

03 / Processing

On-Device Processing 本地化处理

Any lab report scanning (OCR) is performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Images are never uploaded to any external server.

任何化验单扫描（OCR）均使用 Apple 的 Vision 框架在您的设备本地执行。图片绝不会上传到任何外部服务器。

04 / Rights

Your Rights 您的权利

You have the absolute right to modify or delete your data at any time. When you use the "Delete Account" feature in the app settings, all data associations will be permanently removed.

您有权随时修改或删除您的数据。当您在应用设置中使用“注销账号”功能时，所有数据关联都将被永久移除。
