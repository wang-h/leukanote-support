Privacy Policy 隐私政策

Last updated: April 12, 2026 最后更新：2026年4月12日

Your data belongs entirely to you. We do not access, read, share, or sell any of your health data.

您的数据完全归您所有。我们不会获取、阅读、共享或出售您的任何健康数据。

1. Data Storage 1. 数据存储

Leukanote stores all health records (lab data, indicators, notes) exclusively on your iOS device and your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any central servers.

小白随记将所有健康记录（化验数据、指标、笔记）仅存储在您的 iOS 设备和您个人的 iCloud 账户中。我们不运行任何中央服务器。

2. Identity & Privacy 2. 身份与隐私

We use "Sign in with Apple" for secure, anonymous authentication. We do not collect your real name, phone number, or email address.

我们使用“通过 Apple 登录”进行安全、匿名的身份验证。我们不会收集您的真实姓名、手机号或邮箱地址。

3. Local Processing 3. 本地化处理

Lab report recognition (OCR) is performed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision technology. Images are never uploaded to any external server.

化验单识别（OCR）完全基于 Apple 的 Vision 技术在设备本地运行。图片绝不会上传到任何外部服务器。

4. Your Rights 4. 您的权利

You have absolute control over your data. You can delete individual records or your entire account at any time within the app settings.

您对数据拥有绝对控制权。您可以随时在应用设置中删除单条记录或注销整个账号。
