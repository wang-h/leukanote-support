Privacy Policy 隐私政策

Effective Date: April 12, 2026 生效日期：2026年4月12日

Your health records never leave your hands. We do not access, store, or sell your private data. 您的健康记录永远掌握在自己手中。我们不会获取、存储或出售您的私人数据。
01 / Data Storage

Data Storage & Encryption 数据存储与加密

All health data, lab reports, and indicators you enter are stored exclusively on your iPhone device and your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not use any centralized servers. Your data is encrypted using Apple's standard encryption methods both at rest and in transit via iCloud.

您输入的所有健康数据、化验单和指标仅存储在您的 iPhone 设备和您个人的 Apple iCloud 账户中。我们不使用任何中央服务器。您的数据在存储和通过 iCloud 传输过程中均采用 Apple 的标准加密技术。

02 / Data Collection

Information We Collect 我们收集的信息

To provide a complete health tracking experience, the app processes the following types of information:

为了提供完整的健康追踪体验，应用会处理以下类型的信息：

  • Health Metrics: Indicators like blood glucose, blood pressure, etc., manually entered or recognized from reports.健康指标：手动输入或从化验单识别的血糖、血压等指标。
  • Images: Photos of lab reports you choose to scan (processed locally).图片：您选择扫描的化验单照片（在本地处理）。
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous technical data (e.g., language preference, OS version) to improve app stability.应用使用数据：用于提高稳定性的匿名技术数据（如语言偏好、系统版本）。
03 / Authentication

Sign in with Apple 账户与身份

We utilize "Sign in with Apple" for secure and anonymous authentication. We do not collect your real name, phone number, or residential address. If you choose to share your email, it is only used for account identity verification.

我们利用“通过 Apple 登录”进行安全且匿名的身份验证。我们不会收集您的真实姓名、手机号或家庭住址。如果您选择共享电子邮件，它仅用于账户身份验证。

04 / Third-Party Services

Third-Party Services 第三方服务

Leukanote relies on certain Apple services to function:

小白随记依赖某些 Apple 服务来运行：

  • iCloud: For cross-device data synchronization.iCloud：用于多设备间的数据同步。
  • Vision Framework: For on-device OCR (Optical Character Recognition).Vision Framework：用于本地 OCR（文字识别）。
  • App Store: For app distribution and subscription management.App Store：用于应用分发及订阅管理。

Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more details.

更多详情请参阅 Apple 隐私政策。

05 / Processing

On-Device Processing 本地化处理

Any lab report scanning (OCR) is performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Images are never uploaded to any external server. We believe your medical history should remain completely private.

任何化验单扫描（OCR）均使用 Apple 的 Vision 框架在您的设备本地执行。图片绝不会上传到任何外部服务器。我们认为您的病史应完全保持私密。

06 / Children

Children's Privacy 未成年人隐私

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 14 in mainland China). If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

我们不会故意收集 13 周岁（或中国大陆 14 周岁）以下儿童的个人信息。如果家长或监护人发现其子女向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们。

07 / Rights

Your Rights 您的权利

You have the absolute right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time. When you use the "Delete Account" feature in the app settings, all data associations will be permanently removed from our records and your iCloud storage.

您有权随时访问、修改或删除您的数据。当您在应用设置中使用“注销账号”功能时，所有数据关联都将从我们的记录以及您的 iCloud 存储中永久移除。

08 / Contact

Contact Us 联系我们

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:

如果您对我们的隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：
